We offer a comprehensive, one-day Hobby Drone License course designed to equip drone enthusiasts with essential knowledge in drone safety, compliance with Kenya Civil Aviation Authority (KCAA) regulations, and effective operational skills for recreational flying. Our training ensures that each participant gains a strong foundation in responsible drone use.

Course Requirements:
  • • Proficiency in English for clear communication
  • • Physical and mental readiness for drone operation
  • • Possession of a Class 3 medical certificate, ensuring fitness for flying activities

Additionally, we provide a license renewal option with supervised flights for skill refreshment.
