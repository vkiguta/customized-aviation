Our Remote Pilot License (RPL) program is designed for individuals looking to become certified drone pilots in compliance with national aviation standards. The RPL certification provides comprehensive training, covering essential skills in drone operations, safety protocols, and airspace navigation, preparing you for a variety of professional applications.

Covering The Civil Aviation (Unmanned Aircraft Systems) Regulations (2020), this course includes:
  • • Air Law/ KCAA (Unmanned Aircraft Systems) Regulations 2020
  • • UAS General Knowledge and Theory of flight
  • • Meteorology
  • • Navigation and Flight Planning
  • • Human Factors
  • • Navigation and Flight Planning
Course Requirements:
  • • Proficiency in English for clear communication
  • • Physical and mental readiness for drone operation
  • • Possession of a Class 3 medical certificate, ensuring fitness for flying activities
  • • +18yrs of age
