Our RPA (Hobby) Training Program is tailored for drone enthusiasts who want to master the skills to fly remotely piloted aircraft legally and responsibly.

The curriculum covers essential topics, from hands-on piloting techniques to comprehensive regulatory compliance, ensuring participants gain a thorough grounding in the rules and best practices that make safe operation possible. Led by experienced instructors, this program combines practical skills training with insights into the latest industry standards, empowering hobbyists to confidently navigate the skies while adhering to legal guidelines.

For anyone eager to turn their passion for drones into a skill they can use in personal projects, this certification offers the knowledge, confidence, and credentials needed to soar to new heights safely.

This course includes:
  • • Airspace Regulations: Comprehensive overview of Kenyan airspace rules and safety standards.
  • • Hands-on Flight Training: Practical sessions with experienced instructors to build foundational piloting skills.
  • • Safety Protocols & Emergency Handling: Techniques for managing risk and ensuring operational safety in various environments.
  • • Certification Exam: A practical assessment verifies each trainee’s readiness for safe, legal operation.
