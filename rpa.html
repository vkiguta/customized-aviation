Our RPA (Hobby) Certification Program is crafted for drone enthusiasts looking to legally and safely operate remotely piloted aircraft. This certification is ideal for individuals pursuing recreational or professional applications in various fields, with a curriculum designed to cover essential skills and regulatory compliance.

  • • Airspace Regulations: Comprehensive overview of Kenyan airspace rules and safety standards.
  • • Hands-on Flight Training: Practical sessions with experienced instructors to build foundational piloting skills.
  • • Safety Protocols & Emergency Handling: Techniques for managing risk and ensuring operational safety in various environments.
  • • Certification Exam: A practical assessment verifies each trainee’s readiness for safe, legal operation.
Course Requirements:
  • • Proficiency in English for clear communication
  • • Physical and mental readiness for drone operation
  • • Possession of a Class 3 medical certificate, ensuring fitness for flying activities
